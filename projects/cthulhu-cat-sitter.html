Cthulhu’s Cat Sitter

A non-Euclidean 3D horror puzzle game for PC & VR: you take a “simple” cat-sitting job, survive five nights of impossible trials, and return each time to a house that keeps changing. Your only constant rule: feed the cat… or become the food.

Genre: Horror Puzzle Platform: PC + VR Hook: Non-Euclidean trials Loop: 5 nights + house returns Style: PS1-inspired low-poly
